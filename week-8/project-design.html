Project Design

Project Plan

Title
Dealmani
Goal
The goal is to share with people how to purchase items on the Internet at lower price.
Target Audience
Koreans who are currently living in the U.S. or Canada and who are not living in the U.S. but want to order at an online shop in the U.S.
All those are interested in buying products at a lower price.
I’m considering the range of age from high school students to those in their 60s. So I will use simple, easy words and not have complex designs to attract people of broad ages.
Main Features
Photos and price information of the products currently on sale will be provided, links to purchase the products, and online coupons will be uploaded as well.
Personality
Informativ, Trendsetter, Enjoyable
People will get a lot of useful information from the site and enjoy shopping with the information provided.
Limiting Factors
Most of the sales information will be provided is online stores, so the main visitors will be users who are used to shopping the Internet. Such people are usually expected to have a fast Internet connection. Due to the characteristics of users who want to check sales information frequently wherever they are, mobile chrome or Safari users will be expected as a high connection rate. However, people will prefer to check more detail from desktops when they purchase items that are expensive or want to see more accurate colors. Therefore, it’s necessary to use a responsive design that is printed well in both devices. Since the target audience is Koreans, I will use Korean as the main language. English will also be used to post the product information, but Korean will be used mainly on the Website.

    • coupons.html

      - the links that can get a free sample

      - forum that can freely talk to each others

Design Guide

Color Palette

Dark Slate Gray #4E5258
Pale Goldenrod #E3DAAA
White #FFFFFF
Medium Aquamarine #5FBA9C
Light Sea Green #179A6F
Tomato #F8545A

I will use the these colors. High-saturation colors such as tomatoes and sea green will be used as small points such as logo, "new" icon, and buttons. The main color is dark slate grey and white.

Media Assets

General Guidelines

  • Images optimized as JPG using the Photoshop save for web feature to determine the best choice. Photos generally saved as JPG (image-of-product.jpg)
  • I'll use the icons for the navigation bar as svg or gif format (icon-deals.gif, icon-deals.svg)
  • The images of products are taken from their site, but mark the copyright
  • I’ll design the logo using the Photoshop

Styles Guide

Detailed descriptions for CSS

Background

Background is white

{background-color: #FFFFFF;}

Default font

Since I will use Korean as the main language, I will use a Korean-based font such as Nanum Gothic and Myeongjo.
Text is dark slate gray, 14 pixels.

p {
font-family: 'Nanum Gothic', sans-serif;
font-size: 14px;
color: #4E5258;
}

Header

The header section is 140px tall, it will displayed on the top of each pages with the logo. The logo will be placed on the left side, and there’s a search bar in the middle.

header {

background-image: url("../images/logo.png”);
background-repeat: no-repeat;
background-position: top left;
background-size: 140px 80px;
background-position-x: 10%;
background-position-y: 50%;
}
