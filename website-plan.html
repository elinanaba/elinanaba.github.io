Website Plan

1. Website topic and title
The title is “Dealmana”. “Deal” means deals such as hot deals, or good deals when we’re shopping. And the next, “mana” is a Korean word and it means “have a lot of”. I combined these words to express that there’s a lot of deals on our website!
2. Target audience
Koreans who are currently living in the U.S. or Canada
Koreans who are not living in the U.S. but want to order at an online shop in the U.S.
All those are interested in buying items cheaply.
3. Site purpose
A site that collects promotion and coupon information from stores in the U.S. such as Amazon, Bestbuy, and Target. The site will provide useful information about the lowest prices or shopping tips.
4. The URLs for three comparable site examples
Slickdeals https://slickdeals.net/
MissyCoupons https://www.missycoupons.com/
CouponPX https://www.couponpx.com/
5. Main content you intend to include
Deals
Freebies
Stores
Community(Free-talks, Reviews)
